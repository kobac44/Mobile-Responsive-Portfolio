About Me

I live in Durham, North Carolina. I am a World-Renowned Jazz Drummer and have worked as a Professional Jazz Musician for 20+ years.

I am a strong communicator, and it is important to me to convey complete ideas, thoughts, and difficulties. Building teamwork and trust are my strongest attributes, as well as maintaining good time management and skill development, whether working alone or with a team.
